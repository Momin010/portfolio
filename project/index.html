02 · Origin

Both my parents have PhDs in education.
They gave us just enough to live a peaceful life, not everything we wanted. By doing this they forced us to actually try.

When my friends had RC cars and I didn't, I was obsessed with making one. I had no setup: two motors from an old toy, scraps from other projects, cardboard. It wasn't the best. It moved.

Back then I didn't get why they made my life so hard. Now I get it. Whenever I hit a problem it isn't a dead end. It's a challenge, even when my friends quit.
